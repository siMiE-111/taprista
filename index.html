Our Story

From tapri roots to a café worth sitting in

Taprista began with a simple idea: keep the honesty of a roadside tapri — strong tea, unhurried conversation, a place that doesn't ask you to leave — and pair it with the comfort of a proper café. Tucked into Mahalaxmi Nagar in Manewada, it's a compact, 100% vegetarian space where students spread out their notes, friends linger over cold coffee, and professionals take calls between sips.

Everything on the menu is made fresh, kept simple, and priced so that "let's grab a coffee" never needs a second thought.
